How it's made?

Try our chocolates today and discover the perfect balance of flavor

  1. Heat the milk in a saucepan without bringing it to a boil. Add sugar and cocoa powder.
  2. At the same time, prepare a water bath. Melt the butter on it.
  3. Mix the milk mixture and melted butter. Stir thoroughly with a silicone spatula until the mass becomes uniform and silky.
  4. Heat the mixture over low heat, letting it barely boil.
  5. Pour the chocolate into special containers (or use ice molds). Allow to cool and harden.
